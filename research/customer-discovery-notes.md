## Key Interview Questions

### Problem Discovery Questions

1. **Daily Routine:**
   - Walk me through a typical day caring for your child
   - What are the most challenging moments?

2. **Development Tracking:**
   - How do you currently track your child's development?
   - What milestones are you most focused on?

3. **Resource Usage:**
   - What tools or apps do you use for parenting support?
   - What do you like/dislike about them?

4. **Pain Points:**
   - What keeps you up at night regarding your child's development?
   - Where do you feel most uncertain or stressed?

5. **Community & Support:**
   - Who do you turn to for parenting advice?
   - How connected do you feel to other parents?

### Solution Validation Questions

1. **Feature Prioritization:**
   - Which features would be most valuable to you?
   - What would you be willing to pay for?

2. **Usage Patterns:**
   - When would you use this tool?
   - How often would you engage with it?

3. **Barriers to Adoption:**
   - What concerns do you have about using this?
   - What would prevent you from trying it?
